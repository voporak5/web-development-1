Taylor Services Youth Organization's Winter Coat Giveaway is a charitable initiative aimed at providing warm winter coats to children who may not have access to adequate winter clothing. This initiative recognizes the importance of ensuring that all young people are properly equipped to face the harsh winter weather, regardless of their socio-economic status.

We collect new or gently used winter coats from donors throughout the community. These coats are then sorted by size and distributed to children in need. The Winter Coat Giveaway not only provides physical warmth to those who receive a coat, but also sends a message of community support and care. It's a practical way we aim to help children in need and to ensure that all young people can stay warm during the winter months.
