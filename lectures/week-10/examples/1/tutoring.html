Interested in enrolling your child into our tutoring program?

Crystal Taylor

The after school program focuses on reading, math and study skills. The program is designed around a set of ordered skills which the students tackle progressively to achieve better comprehension and basic skills. Program meets every scheduled school day, Monday thru Friday, 6AM to 9AM then resumes from 2PM to 6PM. ILLINOIS ACTION FOR CHILDREN (CCAP) IS ACCEPTED. Transportation service is available.
