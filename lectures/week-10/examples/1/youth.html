The Giving Youth Christmas Celebration is for youth and their families! This is a heartwarming event that aims to bring together young people and their loved ones for a memorable holiday experience.

This inclusive gathering offers a range of activities and entertainment suitable for all ages. Families can enjoy festive games, participate in arts and crafts, and indulge in delicious holiday treats. There may also be live performances, such as music, dance, or a theater production, that showcase the talent of the youth and create a joyful atmosphere.

The celebration provides an opportunity for families to bond, create new memories, and embrace the spirit of togetherness during the Christmas season.
