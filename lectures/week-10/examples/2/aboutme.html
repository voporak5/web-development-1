About Me

Hey there! I'm Vivian Mary Penuelas, currently diving into the exciting world of graphic design and illustration at Columbia College Chicago. Every day is a canvas for creativity as I pursue a BA in Graphic Design with a minor of Illustration on the side.

I'm weaving my creative journey at Columbia. It's not just about the degree; it's about turning my passion into a graphic design and illustration career. ★



★ Little Vivian ★

Let me take you back a bit. Growing up, I was that kid who had a crayon in hand from day one. From creating comic strips with my little brother to illustrating short stories for friends, I lived and breathed art. In 2014, at when I was sixteen, my parents being huge supporters hooked me up with art classes at Waubonsee Community College. Those classes sparked a love for the arts that's stuck with me since.
