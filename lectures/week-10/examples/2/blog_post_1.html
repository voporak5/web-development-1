Final Project Proposal

Published on: December, 14th 2023


In this final project proposal, I articulate my vision for a dynamic website dedicated to showcasing my artistic journey and business, Vivian Mary Co. The proposed website aims to provide a comprehensive experience for visitors, encompassing an "About Me" page, a blog section, a portfolio highlighting my work, and a potential online shop.


Website Structure:


About Me Page:

I envision a detailed "About Me" page that delves deeper into my artistic and business journey. This page will feature a photo of little Vivian from my childhood and a photo carousel showcasing my earlier works.

Contact Page:

The website will have a contact form. Users can choose between general inquiries or business/ommission inquiries through a user-friendly drop-down menu.

Blog Page:

A dedicated blog section will allow me to share insights into my creative process, detailing specific projects (like this one). The page will showcase at least two blog posts.

Shop Page:

I aim to set up a shop page, as a prototype (Etsy won't allow embedding). The shop will display a selection of products, categorized for user convenience, such as a separate button for stickers. Once the "buy now" button is clicked it will lead you to that listing in my Etsy shop

Portfolio Page:

The portfolio page will present my work. Clickable images will lead users to a detailed view of each work, accompanied by brief descriptions.

Mobile-Friendly Design:

As a "cool" element, I want to ensure the website's mobile-friendliness for enhancing the user experience across various devices.

Conclusion:

My final project proposal outlines a visually appealing and user-friendly website that aims to showcase my artistic journey and promote Vivian Mary Co. With a well-thought-out structure, this website promises to captivate visitors and establish a strong online presence for my business. I plan on continuing to work and refine this website and use it as my home website.
