GUILTY GEAR

Mankind knew that they could not change society, so instead of reflecting on themselves, they blamed the beasts. But they saw beauty in the lives of the beasts, and could not lie to themselves about it.

In 1999, an alien force invaded every technology on Earth. In response, humanity banned technology and came to rely upon magic. Not long after, mankind created "Gears": an artifical race of biological weapons that would wage war on humanity for the next thousand years.

Sol Badguy, a Gear man with his own autonomy, has a simple goal: kill the Gears, That Man who created them, and punch through anyone who gets in his way.

Guilty Gear is a fighting game series by Arc System Works that started in 1998. The series has seen five major releases since: Guilty Gear X, Guilty Gear X2, Guilty Gear 2: Overture, Guilty Gear Xrd -SIGN-, and the most recent installment as of 2023, Guilty Gear Strive.

The series boasts fast-paced 2D fighting with a high level of freedom granted by its unique mechanic, the Roman Cancel, which allows a player to correct mistaken inputs, continue combos in unorthodox ways, and more. Series creator Daisuke Ishiwatari imbues his own love for rock and metal into his work to create a high-octane atmosphere.
