Known Issues After Recent Update

12.12.2023

The following issues are scheduled to be fixed.

Platforms: PS4 / PS4 / Xbox Series X|S / Xbox One / Steam / Windows
  • The game becomes unresponsive for a certain time after landing a Finishing Blow after the end of a round with Elphelt (Does not stop the progression of the game)
  • Erratic behavior on certain occasions when using Elphelt's Here I Go! while being connected by Baiken's S Kabari or Ground Throw
  • Unintentional changes are altering Faust's Scarecrow properties
  • Millia's Tandem Top launches a second Tandem Top under certain conditions
  • In Arcade Mode, being thrown by the opposing Faust's Ground Throw would make the player's character invisible under certain conditions
