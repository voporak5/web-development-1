The biggest collection of Star Wars of all time

Steve Sansweet (United States) has collected around 500,000 unique pieces at the Obi-Wan Ranch in northern California. As of May 4, 2015, "only" 93,260 pieces had been properly audited and catalogued, a number four times higher than the previous record holder for this title. Sansweet estimates that the cataloging process will take years to complete as his collection continues to grow.
