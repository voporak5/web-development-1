Before you start...


This is a sort of basic escape room done in HTML. There is no programming at all as I thought it would be fun to limit myself on the capabilites of basic HTML & CSS. It can absolutely be brute forced because of these limitations but please do not do this :)

There is a map, however it is a static image that does not update. However, rooms are marked on the map and your position will be in the tab's title. Use this to keep track of your position!

For the sake of my sanity, there is a lot of reusing of assets and while I can go and comment things out manually for every page, that would take a while! All four buttons for movement will always be present, but they will only have functionality if the movement is valid. For example, if there's a wall on your right, when you click the East button, nothing happens. Keep that in mind if a button does nothing and consult the map! Thank you for reading, and have fun!
